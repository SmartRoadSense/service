About

Who we are

SmartRoadSense is a project of the Scuola di Scienze e Tecnologie dell’Informazione dell’Università di Urbino and of the Associazione Culturale NeuNet. The project has been launched in 2013 and has been partly financed by Fondazione Cassa di Risparmio di Pesaro and Autolinee Vitali.

SmartRoadSense is one of the core components of the Crowd4Roads (C4Rs) project, a three-year European Project funded in the context of the Horizon 2020 programme, coordinated by the University of Urbino, with Regione Marche, Comuto, Coventry University, Buckinghamshire Advantage, Fundatia Satean, and Regione Abruzzo.
